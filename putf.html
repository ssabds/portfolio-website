About

PUTF is a native mobile app providing an all-in-one platform in which young creatives can find new opportunities tailored to their needs and can apply directly to them. The general purpose of this app is to democratize access to resources and provide an intuitive interface which will become embedded in creatives lives without having to exist on social medias.

Problem

Creative people have a hard time finding opportunities. Few competitive apps offer job listing content but none offer other opportunities such as grants, residencies.. People monetize out of this lack of access by creating gated communities and making the access to the resource even more inaccessible. PUTF is responding to a growing need: access to opportunities in the creative economy. I’ve been working on this project since 2020. In fact, PUTF currently exists as an Instagram page in which I share daily opportunities. Although, my community is growing daily, I’m realizing that IG is not a good platform for this kind of service. Users cannot save their listings or apply directly to them. They have to type the URL on their browser to access the web link. This app will solve an existing issue in an existing community.

Competitive Analysis

Competitors such as Indeed or Glassdoor only focus on sharing job listings, whereas PUTF is sharing also grants, residencies and other opportunities that can bring funds to applicants. On the other hand, PUTF is not instrumented by algorithms, all the listings are hand picked. This curatorial aspect makes the app stand out from other competitors disconnected from their users.
